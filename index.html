基于非线性意见动力学的路径规划

Path Planning Inspired By Nonlinear Opinion Dynamics

2024年6月 - 2025年6月 June 2024 - June 2025

导师: 梅文俊教授, 北京大学工学院

Supervisor: Prof. Wenjun Mei, College of Engineering, Peking University

项目概述:

基于非线性意见动力学理论，开发新型机器人路径规划算法。

主要贡献:

  • 对Naomi Leonard教授小组的"可调敏感度的非线性意见动力学"进行深入文献综述
  • 提出将意见-注意力机制集成到机器人路径规划算法中的研究方案
  • 独立学习机器人路径规划，与高年级学生讨论研究方向
  • 复现原始论文的代码并验证实验结果
  • 修改动力学方程，将意见-注意力机制集成到RRT算法中
  • 解决复杂导航场景中的死锁问题

成果与收获:

  • 因自学和探索能力获得导师认可
  • 获得都灵理工大学暑期研究实习的推荐

Project Overview:

Developed novel robot path planning algorithms based on nonlinear opinion dynamics theory.

Key Contributions:

  • Conducted in-depth literature review on "Nonlinear Opinion Dynamics with Tunable Sensitivity" from Professor Naomi Leonard's group
  • Proposed research scheme integrating opinion-attention mechanisms into robot path planning algorithms
  • Independently studied robot path planning and discussed research directions with senior students
  • Reproduced the original paper's code and verified experimental results
  • Modified dynamics equations to integrate opinion-attention mechanism into RRT algorithm
  • Addressed deadlock issues in complex navigation scenarios

Achievements:

  • Recognized for self-learning and exploration capabilities by advisor
  • Received recommendation for summer research internship at Politecnico di Torino

社会变革的简单模型

A Simple Model of Social Change

2025年7月 - 至今 July 2025 - Present

导师: Giacomo Como教授, 都灵理工大学

Supervisor: Prof. Giacomo Como, Politecnico di Torino

项目概述:

开发数学模型研究社会变革过程中的意见传播和群体行为动态。

主要贡献:

  • 研究意见动力学中的孵化现象
  • 通过广泛的建模和模拟发现新的动力学特性
  • 与导师保持频繁会议（每周至少3次）确保研究进展
  • 访问伦敦帝国理工学院和格罗宁根大学进行学术交流
  • 与Dario Paccagnan教授、Ming Cao教授及其博士生讨论项目
  • 交流优化和深化研究的想法

成果与收获:

  • 拓宽对控制理论和群体动力学的理解
  • 展示了强大的数学建模能力
  • 巩固了对理论研究和方法开发的兴趣

Project Overview:

Developed mathematical models to study opinion propagation and group behavior dynamics in social change processes.

Key Contributions:

  • Investigated incubation phenomenon in opinion dynamics
  • Discovered new dynamic properties through extensive modeling and simulations
  • Maintained frequent meetings (at least 3 times per week) with advisor to ensure research progress
  • Visited Imperial College London and University of Groningen for academic exchange
  • Discussed project with Professor Dario Paccagnan, Professor Ming Cao, and their PhD students
  • Exchanged ideas on optimizing and deepening the research

Achievements:

  • Broadened understanding of control theory and group dynamics
  • Demonstrated strong mathematical modeling capabilities
  • Solidified interest in theoretical research and method development

多智能体协作的高考复习系统

National College Entrance Examination Review System with Multi-Agent Collaboration

导师: 袁坤教授

Supervisor: Professor Kun Yuan

  • 设计并实现了一种新颖的多智能体系统，用于高考复习，利用三个专门的LLM智能体，具有不同的角色：学习者、教师和助教
  • 使用Tkinter开发了一个全面的GUI界面，支持API配置、科目选择、文档上传和实时对话显示
  • 设计了PDF和DOCX格式的文档解析功能
  • 实现了一个结构化的对话工作流程，学习者智能体根据上传的材料提出问题，教师智能体提供解释，助教智能体生成总结性的学习图表
  • 集成了DeepSeek API（用于推理密集型任务）和GPT API（用于总结任务），以优化性能和成本效益
  • 开发了将对话日志保存为JSON文件的功能，用于后续分析和复习，增强了系统对纵向学习跟踪的实用性
  • Designed and implemented a novel multi-agent system for National College Entrance Examination review, utilizing three specialized LLM agents with distinct roles: Learner, Teacher, and Teaching Assistant
  • Developed a comprehensive GUI interface using Tkinter that supports API configuration, subject selection, document upload, and real-time conversation display
  • Engineered document parsing capabilities for both PDF and DOCX formats
  • Implemented a structured conversation workflow where the Learner agent raises questions based on uploaded materials, the Teacher agent provides explanations, and the Teaching Assistant agent generates summarized study charts and tables
  • Integrated DeepSeek API (for reasoning-intensive tasks) and GPT API (for summarization tasks) to optimize performance and cost-effectiveness
  • Developed functionality to save conversation logs as JSON files for subsequent analysis and review, enhancing the system's utility for longitudinal learning tracking
